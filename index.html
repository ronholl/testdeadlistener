INSTALL THE APP
Browse and play every Grateful Dead show right from your home screen — no App Store, no browser chrome, just music.

iPhone · Safari only:
1  Tap the Share ⎙ button at the bottom
2  Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
3  Tap Add — done ✓

Android · Chrome:
Tap ⋮ Menu → Add to Home screen
What's Here
🎵 Shows — all — Dead, Jerry, Phil and Bob concerts available in the archive. Filter by year, search venue, city or date. Tap any show for its setlist & player
🔍 Songs — type any song to find every show it was played. Tap a result to jump straight there
💀 Deadheads — other Deadheads show lists. Tap a name to browse their attended shows
🎲 Jam — discover shows: Favorites, On This Day, and Play a Show
The Jam Tab
⭐ Favorites — starred shows from all listeners. Your favorites pin to the top of your list (max 5)
📅 On This Day — shows from today's date across all years, sorted by rating
🎲 Play a Show — roll the dice for a surprise, weighted toward top rated shows
Reading the Show List
▶ Green arrow — audio available on Relisten
SBD Gold badge — soundboard recording (best quality)
Colored circles — which Deadheads attended this show
+ button — mark as a show you attended
My shows filter — see only your attended shows
